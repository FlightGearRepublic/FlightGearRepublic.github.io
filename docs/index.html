FlightGear Republic

About

FlightGear Republic is a community of sim pilots flying with the Open Source flight simulator FlightGear and aviation enthusiasts.

Joining the community

There are several ways of joining and interacting with the community. You can find FlightGear Republic on social media and in chat platforms.

Join us on chat platforms.

Matrix

Matrix is an open source protocal for group collaboration. People join rooms that are parts of communities.

Join us on Matrix

There are several free Matrix clients you can use, just pick one and use the one you like. We recommend Element, Hydrogen and Fluffly Chat. Use this link to join!

Discord

Discord is one of the major group chat platforms, you can only use its official client, which is closed source, to talk on Discord.

Join us on Discord

To join you need an "invite link". Click on this link to join our Discord server.
